Sample from the UK Observatories Year Book
==========================================

The `Observatories Year Book <http://brohan.org/UK-digitisation/sources/observatories.html>`_, available as `digital images from the National Met Library and Archive <https://digital.nmla.metoffice.gov.uk/collection_5575296f-0406-49f5-89cb-54cd79486b75/>`_, is a high priority candidate for an automatic transcription system as it is printed tables of data, with high-quality images available.
